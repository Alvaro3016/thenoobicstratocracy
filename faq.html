Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I join a division? Each division has different requirements. Some require tryouts, others applications or volunteering.
  • How do I get promoted? Promotions are based on merit, time in group, activity, reputation, and recommendations from NCOs or Officers.
  • Can you join multiple divisions? Yes.
  • I can't verify my account! Make sure you are in the group, follow the bot's instructions, and try again.
  • What to do if there's an exploiter? Call a moderator, collect evidence, and avoid interacting with the exploiter.
  • Where are tryouts hosted? In the deployments channel, at the discretion of officers.
