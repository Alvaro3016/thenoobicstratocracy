Divisions

  • 1NF: First Noobic Fleet (naval branch, includes Naval Air Corps & Marine Corps)
  • 2NIA: 2nd Noobic Intelligence Agency
  • 3ND: 3rd Noob Division (large manpower, Americas timezone)
  • NAC: Noobic Armored Corps (tanks and vehicles)
  • NOOBSOC: Noobic Special Operations Command (elite, skilled units)
  • 6NLA: 6th Noobic Logistics Agency (design, funding, uniforms)
  • NTC: Noobic Training Command (training and recruitment)
  • 8NEC: 8th Noobic Engineering Corps (development, maps, assets)
  • SMP: Stratocratic Military Police (law enforcement)
  • GSMC: Ground Service Medical Corps (medical support)
