Noobic Combat Uniform (NCU)

The standard uniform for all members, worn at raids, trainings, and events. Customization is allowed to a degree, but lewd or provocative items are not permitted. Animal ears are allowed. Each division may have its own combat uniform.

Noobic Formals

The pride of an infantryman's wardrobe. Must be worn with the Noobic Beret (black berets for 5NSO). Only ribbons are allowed as t-shirts. Accessories are restricted to facial accessories and animal ears.
