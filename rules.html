Army Rules & Discipline

  • No unnecessary spamming. Chains and repetitions are allowed, but do not clog conversations or chats with spam.
  • NO NSFW/NSFL. Posting such content will result in an immediate ban.
  • Do not disrespect other members or officers. Insults are allowed in context, but hate speech is not tolerated.
  • Advertisement of servers/groups is not allowed unless permitted or affiliated.
  • Do not break any laws or Discord Terms of Service. No SWAT, DDoS, Dox, or malicious links.
  • Do not try to bypass the rules with loopholes.
