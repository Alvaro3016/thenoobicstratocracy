About the Stratocracy

The Noobic Stratocracy — also known as East Noobia — occupies the eastern half of Noobia after a coup and the Bloxian invasion. As a stratocracy, the military is the state: institutions, law, and leadership are shaped by uniformed command. The nation maintains large armed forces, naval power, and a near-fanatical patriotism among its people. The country pursues the War of Reunification against the western Federal Republic of Noobia.

History at a glance

  • Founded as a revival of the 3rd Noob Division (original group from 2015)
  • Experienced multiple shutdowns and revivals; reorganized into a stratocracy in 2025
  • Strong focus on lore, worldbuilding, and community-driven operations

Divisions

Each division serves a distinct role within the Stratocracy.

1NF — Noobic Fleet

Naval branch; includes Naval Air Corps & Marine Corps

Primary naval operations, fleet logistics, and maritime combat. Overseen by the Grand Admiral.

NOOBSOC — Special Operations

Elite operators for specialised missions

Small, highly skilled units focusing on precision engagements and advanced systems.

NAC — Armored Corps

Armour and vehicular combat

Tanks, armoured vehicles, and mechanized support units.

NTC — Training Command

Recruiting & training of enlisted personnel

Handles education, basic training, and specialist courses.

SMP — Military Police

Law enforcement & discipline

Maintains order in service and implements Stratocracy regulations.

GSMC — Medical Corps

Battlefield medical support

Medical teams, triage, and roleplay medical services.

FAQ

What are the requirements to join?

Requirements vary by division. Some require tryouts, others applications. Check each division page or ask recruitment staff.

What training is provided?

Basic training, role-based drills, and specialist courses are run by NTC and division commands.

How do I rank up?

Promotions are based on merit, time served, activity and recommendations from your superiors.

How to report exploiters?

Collect evidence, contact moderators via the Justice Department server, and call a modcall if needed.

Rules & Discipline

General Conduct

  • No spam or purposefully clogging channels.
  • No NSFW/NSFL content — immediate ban.
  • No hateful slurs (racial, homophobic, transphobic). Certain soft uses are still discouraged.
  • No doxxing, swatting, DDoS, or illegal activities.

Uniforms & Etiquette

Combat uniforms are used for trainings and operations; formals have stricter requirements. Accessories should follow the Cold War / 1960s aesthetic where possible.
